First Login

Enter your Username and password. It is recommended to change your password upon your first login. Therefore go to Other in the "Main menu" and choose {% if user.has_usable_password %} "Change password" {% endif %}. When you are done, you are all set to use the database.

Adding Measurements

In the "Main menu", go to Add and choose the measurement method you just used. First select the sample you worked with, then fill out the rest of the form and click on send.


To add a jpg, png or pdf of your data go to "Add result". From your computer choose the file you want to add. Also add a short comment or word, so it becomes more convenient for others to follow your work. If everything ran as usual and nothing nothing noteworthy occured, just type a word or simply 'ok'. But do not keep the comment field empty. This is to make reading the samlplesheets as intuitive as possible.

Adding samples

Simply select "Add samples in the Main Menu. That's it.

Topics

Simply select "Add new Topic" in the Main Menu.
Topics are the projects you're working on. So if you want to stay up to date on a topic and see all the samples that are being added to it, go to "Edit preferences" and enable auto-addition for the topics you're working on. Samples will now be updated automatically in your timeline if they are added by another User. This doesn't happen by default to save you from confusion by unwillingly overloading your collection of samples.

Basic hints

You can fold and hide the listed samles in "Main menu". If folded in, they don't show up in your suggestions when e.g. selecting the samples for a meaurement. If folded out, the samples do show up all the time. Choose what you like the best.

Sharing your samples with other users

In the main menu select the big fat header "Samples". Then you can change and share whatever you want, e.g. select a new currently responsible person to share your data with.

There's a trick when you want to select multiple samples, eventually if they are not next to each other in the Dropdown-Menu. Just select the samples while holding down 'Control' (German keyboard: Strg).

Errors

If the Website throws an Error, reload in your Browser.

Freshly added measurements or results might not be updated instantaneously, then try hitting the Reload Button in your browser again or hit the Refresh Button when using the sample-site (Krankenakte). If still problems occur , try emptying the cache on your browser and load again.
Theses hints may work most of the times. When there are severe problems when loading measurement-files, it might be that the site and its code has recently been updated. For problems, please contact Philip.

Shortcut keys

On your keyboard there are shortcuts to simplify your day-to-day work with the database.

  • Press 'm' + 'Alt' to get back home.
  • Press 'a' + 'Alt' to add a sample.
  • Press 'r' + 'Alt' to add a result.
  • Press 's' + 'Alt' to search for things.
  • Press 'h' + 'Alt' to get help.
  • Press 'l' + 'Alt' to log out.
These key combinations are the way to go when using Microsoft Edge. If you are using Firefox, just add 'Shift' to the keys above.
